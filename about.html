500 words about me

Born and raised in Los Angeles California, daughter of entrepreneur and educator. After graduating from Scripps College with a Bachelor of Arts in Foreign Languages (Spanish and French), I took a brief dip in the fashion world to satisfy my urge to learn how to make clothing professionally (which incidentally triggered learning about Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and color theory), then headed east on what turned out to be a permanent detour to my father's hometown of Chicago.

Almost immediately after arriving in Chicago I landed a job as administrative coordinator at Stuart School of Business at Illinois Institute of Technology. And there I stayed, accumulating skills in many areas, acquiring a diverse skillset as my duties that began in general administration, grew to include event coordination, design, student assistant management, project management, and ... I currently manage operations at one of the campuses after the majority of the school moved to the university's other location. I took advantage of the educational opportunities and am currently finishing up my Master's in Technical Communication and Information Design at Illinois Institute of Technology. Deciding to pursue this degree was a natural progression in my ever-present desire to facilitate information transfer and communication.

My bio, as is my life, is a work in progress. I'm always learning, growing, and reflecting. Come back periodically if you're interested in updates or changes as I continue to evaluate what is "real" and "pretty" for me now.
