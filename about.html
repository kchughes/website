Bio

Born and raised in Los Angeles California, daughter of entrepreneur and educator. After graduating from Scripps College with a Bachelor of Art in Foreign Languages (Spanish and french), I took a brief dip in the fashion world to satisfy my urge to learn how to make clothing professionally (which incidentally began my path in color theory, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and color theory), then headed west on what turned out to be a permanent detour to my father's hometown, Chicago.

I am currently finishing up my Master's in Technical Communication and Information Design at Illinois Institute of Technology. I view deciding to pursue this degree was a natural progression in my ever-present desire to facilitate information transfer and communication.

My bio is a work in progress, as is my life. I'm always learning, growing, and reflecting. Come back periodically if you're interested in updates or changes as I continue to evaluate what is "real and pretty" for me now.
