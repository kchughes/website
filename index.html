Me in 238 words

Hi, my name is Khalila and welcome to my personal internet hub. I'm a self-proclaimed jane-of-all trades having worked in various aspects of writing, editing, design, project coordination, and event planning. The three common denominators in everything I've done is the strong desire to make things real, make things pretty, and make things happen. My highly analytical, data-driven, planning left brain and my creative dynamic right brain have come to a truce along the fact that I am a methodical creative problem solver. Every aspect of the real, pretty, and happen trifecta involves creative problem solving that comes with making something that is real by taking into account everyday user uses and considerations, pretty, keeping the design as approachable and inviting as the content should be, and bringing it all together to make it happen however possible because, well, otherwise, what's the point. I've worked in a variety of roles and in every one

So, what does this all mean? I highly analytical, always asking questions, and a planner, keeping my checklist in hand, but I can easily throw the checklists aside when they no longer serve the purpose or it just needs to get done. I especially those that get to the heart of an issue. facilitated by a collaborative take, be it with a client or project team, I'm a team player but know how to get things done.

Whew, now 238 words later...if you haven't read enough about me, i've got another 500 words for you in the about page. Otherwise, please feel free to peruse my work or resume at will. I'm also always available for questions or further discussions via the handy contact page.
