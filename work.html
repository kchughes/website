Work Approach

Make it Real

Here's where it starts and where it should always start. With the 'real' of everyday, whether it's the content, audience, or goal. 'Real' is the information you want to convey, as well as the audience you want to convey it to, to attain a specific objective. 'Real' is not the same for everyone or for every project, so defining what it is is an ever-changing and often challenging process that involves first asking the right questions.

Make it Pretty

It's not a coincidence that functional design is usually quite pleasing to the eye. Highly effective design makes the information it is transforming accessible and inviting.

Make it Happen

The real and the pretty interact with each other in a strange dance in which one is always informing the other. These are just some of the aspects that come along when thinking about a project. In the end, frequent communication and dynamic collaborations are needed to keep everything on track to not only make it happen but also to exceed expectations.

Repeat

Life is not stagnant and neither is the information or design trends that inform our everyday lives. The real changes as much as it needs to and, because it is tied to the real, so too can design. Regular evaluations must be made to make sure that projects are real and pretty in the now.
