Work Approach

Make it Real

Here's where it starts and where it should always start. With the real of everyday, be it the content, audience, or goal. Real is the information you want to convey, to the audience you want to convey it to, to attain the you created when you created it. Real is not the same for everyone or for every project, so defining what it is a ever-changing and often challenging process.

Make it Pretty

Some may call this design but, for many, the term design is too intimidating. Everybody who creates and presents information in any form is a designer in their own right. However, the level of success usually involves a set of skills that result in the effectiveness of the . Effective and functional design makes the information . It could be bad, or good, but it should be pretty.

Make it Happen

The real and the pretty interact with each in a strange dance that one is always informing the other. They should be thought of together and put together. Sometimes this gets lost along the way to the detriment of one or the other. It doesn't have to be this way. With the right mindset and collaborations, it can happen to create and distribute a finished product where one feeds into the other and you can't imagine it another way.

Repeat

Life is not stagnant and neither is the information or design trends that inform our everyday lives. Real changes as much as it needs to and because they are often a unite, so too can design. Everything evolves so evaluations must be made to make sure that the product is real and pretty in the now.
